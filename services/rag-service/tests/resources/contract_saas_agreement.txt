1.1 "Platform" means the Provider's cloud-based document management software, including all updates and enhancements.

1.3 "User" means an individual authorized by Customer to access and use the Platform.

4.1 Data Ownership. Customer retains all rights to Customer Data uploaded to the Platform.

4.2 Data Processing. Provider will process Customer Data only as necessary to provide the Platform services.

4.3 Security. Provider maintains industry-standard security measures including:

    (b) Multi-factor authentication
    (c) Regular security audits

ACME Corporation Employee Leave Policy
Document Version: 2.1
Last Updated: January 2025

1. ANNUAL LEAVE ENTITLEMENT

1.1 Standard Entitlement
All full-time employees are entitled to 25 days of paid annual leave per calendar year. Part-time employees receive a pro-rated entitlement based on their contracted hours.

1.2 Accrual
Annual leave accrues at a rate of 2.08 days per month worked. New employees may use accrued leave after completing their probationary period of 3 months.

1.3 Carry Forward
Employees may carry forward up to 5 unused leave days to the next calendar year. Carried forward leave must be used by March 31st of the following year.

2. SICK LEAVE

2.1 Entitlement
Employees are entitled to up to 10 days of paid sick leave per year. Sick leave does not accumulate year to year.

2.2 Notification
Employees must notify their direct manager by 9:00 AM on the first day of absence. A medical certificate is required for absences exceeding 3 consecutive days.

2.3 Long-term Illness
For absences exceeding 10 days, employees may apply for extended medical leave. Extended leave is reviewed on a case-by-case basis by HR.

3. PARENTAL LEAVE

3.1 Maternity Leave
Primary caregivers are entitled to 16 weeks of paid maternity leave at full salary. An additional 10 weeks of unpaid leave may be taken.

3.2 Paternity Leave
Secondary caregivers are entitled to 4 weeks of paid paternity leave at full salary.

3.3 Adoption Leave
Adoptive parents receive the same entitlements as biological parents, beginning from the date of placement.

4. BEREAVEMENT LEAVE

4.1 Immediate Family
Employees are entitled to 5 days of paid bereavement leave for the death of an immediate family member (spouse, parent, child, sibling).

4.2 Extended Family
Employees are entitled to 2 days of paid bereavement leave for the death of an extended family member (grandparent, aunt, uncle, cousin).

5. REQUESTING LEAVE

5.1 Annual Leave Requests
All annual leave requests must be submitted through the HR portal at least 2 weeks in advance. Requests for peak periods (December, Summer) should be submitted 4 weeks in advance.

5.2 Approval Process
Leave requests are reviewed by the employee's direct manager within 3 business days. Requests may be denied based on operational requirements.

5.3 Cancellation
Approved leave may be cancelled with 48 hours notice. Cancellation of leave during peak periods may not be possible.

For questions about this policy, contact hr@acmecorp.com or extension 4567.
